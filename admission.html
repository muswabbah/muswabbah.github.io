Admission Process

Begin your journey of Islamic knowledge at Jamiul Muswabbah. Learn about our straightforward admission process and requirements.

Apply Now

How to Apply

Our admission process is designed to be simple and transparent. Follow these steps to join our esteemed institution.

1

Inquiry & Information

Contact our admission office to learn more about our programs, schedule a campus visit, or get your questions answered. You can call, email, or visit us in person.

2

Application Submission

Complete the application form available online or at our campus. Submit all required documents along with the application before the deadline.

3

Entrance Assessment

Depending on the program, applicants may need to take an assessment test to evaluate their current knowledge level and determine appropriate placement.

4

Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview with our admission committee. This helps us understand your goals and ensures our program is the right fit for you.

5

Admission Decision

Successful applicants will receive an admission offer via email or post. You'll need to confirm your acceptance by the specified deadline.

6

Registration & Orientation

Complete the registration process, submit required fees, and attend the orientation program to familiarize yourself with the campus and curriculum.

Admission Requirements

Documents Needed for Application

  • Completed application form
  • Recent passport-size photographs (2 copies)
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Previous academic records/transcripts
  • Transfer certificate from previous institution (if applicable)
  • Conduct certificate from previous institution
  • Copy of Aadhaar card or other government ID
  • Medical fitness certificate

Eligibility Criteria

  • Hifz Program: Minimum age of 7 years, basic Arabic reading skills
  • Islamic Studies: Completion of primary education, basic understanding of Islamic principles
  • Arabic Language: No prior knowledge required for beginner levels
  • Youth Program: Age between 13-18 years, interest in Islamic learning

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum age for admission?

The minimum age for our Hifz program is 7 years. For other programs, please refer to the specific eligibility criteria or contact our admission office.

Are there any scholarships available?

Yes, we offer merit-based and need-based scholarships for deserving students. Details are available in the financial aid section of the application form.

Can international students apply?

Yes, we welcome international students. Additional documentation may be required for visa processing. Please contact our international admission office for details.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

We maintain a low student-teacher ratio of 15:1 to ensure personalized attention and effective learning.

Is hostel accommodation available?

Yes, we have separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with comfortable accommodation and dining services.
